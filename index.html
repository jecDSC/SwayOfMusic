The Sway of music

1. We have done a few things, mainly pertaining to cleaning the dataset. For instance, the dataset we picked, which contained the swaying data from participants under different conditions, was too big to push onto github, so we removed many of the participants since the dataset was too large. Additionally, the rows were all 1000th of a second, so we condensed the data to also clean and shorten the data. For the visualizations, we have decided to come up with one main visualization and a few supporting to showcase the data and the overall message of our visualization. Our visualization shows how a participant moves in their body in the VR environment

The most challenging part of the project will be coming up with a cohesive design for the website to showcase our narrative. We want to showcase the sway of people to show the effect of music and environment on the sway of participants, but currently we are not too sure on how to effectively communicate this idea. Additionally, trying to come up with multiple interactive visualizations to fully showcase the data of people swaying is another challenge, since we are limited to showing the swaying data, which only has data on people swaying and nothing more, so it is hard to come up with additional visualizations for this data. Finally, the most difficult challenge is the main visualization, and making sure it is interactive and presentable and professional is the biggest challenge.
